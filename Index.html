About Me

I am a Hardware & Systems Engineering Consultant with extensive expertise in EV charging systems, power electronics, and hardware design. Based in Windsor, Ontario, I provide consulting services to clients across Canada and the United States.

My career spans multiple industries including automotive electronics, medical devices, industrial systems, and telecommunications. I specialize in translating complex requirements into practical, efficient hardware solutions that meet rigorous industry standards.
